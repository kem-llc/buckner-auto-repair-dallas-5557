Our story

Dallas’s Trusted Auto Repair Since 1995

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair that keeps our community safe and mobile.

Buckner Auto Repair was founded in 1995 by master mechanic John Buckner with a simple mission: offer reliable, affordable car care with integrity. What started as a two-bay shop on Buckner Blvd has grown into a full-service facility serving thousands of Dallas drivers. John’s commitment to transparency and craftsmanship built a loyal customer base that spans generations.

Today, our team of ASE-certified technicians carries on that legacy. We invest in the latest diagnostic technology and continuous training to handle everything from routine maintenance to complex repairs. Every job is backed by our satisfaction guarantee because we treat every car as if it were our own.
