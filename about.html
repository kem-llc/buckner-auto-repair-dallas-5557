Our story

Family-Owned & Community-Focused

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our customers safe and satisfied. We treat every car as if it were our own.

Buckner Auto Repair was founded with a simple mission: provide reliable, trustworthy auto repair in Dallas. With over 15 years of experience, our team of ASE-certified mechanics has built a reputation for excellence. We invest in the latest tools and ongoing training to ensure we can handle any vehicle challenge.

As a family-owned business, we treat our customers like neighbors. We believe in transparency, fair pricing, and building long-term relationships. Whether you need a quick oil change or a major engine repair, you can count on us to get the job done right.
